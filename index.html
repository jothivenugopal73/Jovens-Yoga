Common questions

Everything you need to know.

Is my card charged during the free trial?
+
No. Your card is saved securely when you sign up but nothing is charged until day 8. If you cancel before day 8, you pay nothing. Not a single cent.
How do I cancel?
+
Simply send us a WhatsApp message on the same number you received your welcome message from. Our team responds within 2 hours. No forms, no portals, no runaround — just a message and we handle everything.
What if I miss a class?
+
Your teacher notices when you are not there — that is the whole point. Premium members can attend any of the 8 daily slots on any day, so missing your usual slot is never a problem. Standard members have one fixed slot, but your teacher will reach out if you miss two classes in a row.
What timezone are the classes in?
+
Classes are available in your local timezone — the schedule on this page auto-detects where you are and shows your local times. We offer 4 morning and 4 evening slots daily, Monday through Saturday, so there is always a time that works for your schedule.
I am a complete beginner. Is this right for me?
+
Absolutely. Most of our students start as complete beginners. The class size is small — 25 to 30 students — and your teacher gives live corrections by name. You are never invisible, never left behind. Beginners actually do best here because they build habits correctly from day one.
What equipment do I need?
+
A yoga mat, a device with a camera, and a stable internet connection. That is it. No props, no special clothing, no studio. Just you, your mat, and your teacher live on screen.
Can someone join the class with me?
+
Yes — Premium members get one permanent guest who can join every class with them. This is great for couples, roommates, or a parent and child practicing together. Standard members attend solo, but upgrading to Premium is just $10 more per month.
Are the classes recorded? Can I watch them later?
+
Classes are live — that is the whole point. The magic of Jovens is that your teacher sees you, corrects you, and knows when you are not there. Recordings cannot do that. We do not offer on-demand playback, and we never will — because passive streaming is exactly what we are not.
How does the monthly progress report work?
+
At the end of each month your teacher sends you a personal note — what you achieved, what you are working on, and what to focus on next. It is sent via WhatsApp. It is not a generic email. Your teacher writes it based on your actual classes that month.
Is there a long-term contract?
+
None at all. Month to month by default. If you want to save money, we offer 20% off on a 6-month plan and 30% off on an annual plan — but those are optional. You can cancel anytime with a simple WhatsApp message.
